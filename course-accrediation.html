Course Accrediation

Benefit of Accreditation

Credentials awarded by the BHRC are recognized worldwide, including those awarded to persons by universities, organizations, educational institutions, and training facilities. The most important benefits that an Accredited course will enjoy are:

  • Accredited courses add more value to the certificates than those that are not accredited.
  • Accredited courses are accepted globally by the corporates.
  • Accreditation of any course ensures the credibility of the professional certification.
  • Accreditation of any course ensures the professional and perfect assessment of competencies.
  • Accreditation Ensures 10X speed of growth for any course.

Accreditation Process

It is our goal to make this procedure as simple as possible for you to follow and finish. Make sure your legal status is up to date as per the requirement of your country. The process of accreditation is as below:

Step-1

2
Step 2

3
Step 3

Step 4
