Institute Accrediation

Benefit of Accredited Centre

Accreditation is very essential for any institute to prove the quality and acceptability of its courses across the globe. By using the BHRC stamp, any institute can easily establish the quality and standard of its courses and can grow faster with sustainability

  • Getting accreditation establishes the brand value of the accredited Institute which ensures better acceptability
  • The Faculty must have adequate knowledge, experience & expertise to facilitate sessions accredited by BHRC.
  • Accreditation of any course ensures the credibility of the professional certification.
  • Accreditation of any course ensures the professional and perfect assessment of competencies.
  • Accreditation Ensures 10X speed of growth for any course.

Accreditation Process

It is our goal to make this procedure as simple as possible for you to follow and finish. Make sure your legal status is up to date as per the requirement of your country. The process of accreditation is as below:

1
Step-1

Email us info@hrboard.uk to get the application form

2
Step 2

Fill up the form and submit to us.

3
Step 3

BHRC accreditation board will review and if required they will communicate

4
Step 4

Once the BHRC accreditation board is satisfied with the documents and process, they may conduct an on-site or online visit to your site

5
Step 5

Once the visit is satisfactory BHRC will issue the invoice

By requiring institutions to fulfil and maintain their high standards, accreditation fosters the public's trust in them and increases their commitment to transparency and good governance.
